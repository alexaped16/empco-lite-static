Product Description

Model 2006 + Solar Assist

Part # 99-02006+, 99-02006R+

Specification:

  • Internal solar panels trickle charge D-cell Alkaline batteries to give a superior long lasting more reliable power source.
  • Battery life: 19-20 months on 4 alkaline D-cell batteries.
  • Solar panels are internal, providing less chance for damage and theft.
  • Candles from Model 2006 and 2006+ Solar Assist are interchangeable.
  • Meets current MUTCD and I.T.E standards tested by an approved certified laboratory.
  • Available in Red color lens LED.
  • Yellow base is standard, other colors available upon request.
  • Box Quantity: 10 per box.
  • Boxes per pallet: 30
