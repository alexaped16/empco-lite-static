Product Description

Model 400 LED PC

Part # 99-00411L, 99-00421L(Red Lens)

Specification:

  • Comes standard with a 3-way switch with photocell.
  • Uses either one or two 6-volt alkaline batteries.
  • Battery life: 3 months on two 6-volt alkaline batteries.

  • Available in Red color lens.
  • Yellow base is standard, other colors available upon request.
